1. Upcoming Fundraiser: Walk for Education - September 2024

Join us for our upcoming "Walk for Education" fundraiser! Scheduled for September 15th, 2024, this event
will raise funds to support Asibambane's educational programs, including scholarships for local students and
for our newly opened Community Computer Center. Participants will walk a scenic 5km route through
the community, and all proceeds will go directly to our educational initiatives.

2. Asibambane Celebrates 10 Years of Community Service - June 2024

This year marks the 10th anniversary of Asibambane NGO! Over the past decade, we have grown from a small
volunteer group to a full-fledged organization, making a significant impact in the lives of countless community members.
To celebrate this milestone, we hosted a community event featuring speeches from our founders, performances by
